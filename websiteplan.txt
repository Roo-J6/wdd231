
Site Name: Mountain Explorer Hub  
Mountain Explorer Hub was selected as the site name because it represents a central platform for outdoor enthusiasts who love to explore mountainous regions. It provides a hub of information, resources, and community engagement for hikers, climbers, and nature lovers.

Site Purpose:  
Mountain Explorer Hub aims to provide detailed information on various mountain trails, gear recommendations, safety tips, and outdoor adventure guides. The website will also serve as a community space where users can share their experiences, photos, and hiking itineraries.

Scenarios:  
1. What are the best hiking trails for beginners in the Rocky Mountains?  
2. Where can I find expert reviews on hiking and climbing gear?

Color Schema:
- Primary Color: Forest Green (#228B22) - Used for headings and call-to-action buttons.  
- Secondary Color: Earthy Brown (#8B4513) - Used for backgrounds and accents.  
- Neutral Color: Light Beige (#F5F5DC) - Used for body text and content backgrounds.  

Typography:
- Heading Font: 'Montserrat', sans-serif - Used for titles and headings.  
- Body Font: 'Roboto', sans-serif - Used for paragraphs and general content.  
- Accent Font: 'Playfair Display', serif - Used for quotes and testimonials.  

Wireframe:
- Mobile View: The homepage will feature a simple vertical layout with a navigation menu, a featured image, and quick access to trail guides.  
- Desktop View: A wider layout with a sidebar for recent posts, a central section for featured articles, and a navigation bar at the top.
